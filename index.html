What is this?

This is simple web application to count the score of tic-tac-toe, rock-paper-scissors or any other two players' game in which keeping track of scores may seem to be very tedious.

How to use?

To start, set a 'playing to' first which is the winning score. Then add points to players using their corresponding buttons. The first player who reaches the winning score wins. Click the 'Reset' button to play again.
